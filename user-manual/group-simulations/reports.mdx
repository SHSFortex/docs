# Simulation Reports

After completing a simulation, detailed reports provide insights into individual and team performance.

## Accessing Reports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Simulations">
    Go to **Simulations** or **My Training**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Completed Simulation">
    Look in **Completed** tab
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Simulation">
    Open the simulation details
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Report">
    Click **View Report** or **Reports** tab
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Report Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Individual Report">
    **Your Performance**

    - Your decisions and scores
    - Response times
    - Resources accessed
    - Personal feedback
    - Certificate (if earned)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Team Report">
    **Collective Performance**

    - Team score
    - Collaboration metrics
    - Communication analysis
    - Collective decisions
    - Team comparison to benchmarks
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Facilitator Report">
    **Comprehensive View**

    - All individual performances
    - Team dynamics
    - Participation levels
    - Simulation timeline
    - Detailed analytics
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Performance Metrics

### Overall Score

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Your Score" icon="bullseye">
    Total points earned out of maximum possible
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Score" icon="users">
    Collective team performance
  </Card>
  <Card title="Percentile" icon="chart-line">
    How you compare to others
  </Card>
  <Card title="Grade" icon="graduation-cap">
    Letter grade or pass/fail status
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Score Breakdown

**Decision Quality** (typically 60-70% of score)
- Points for each decision
- Optimal vs actual choices
- Rationale evaluation

**Response Time** (typically 10-20%)
- Speed of decision-making
- Balanced with quality

**Collaboration** (typically 10-20%)
- Communication frequency
- Team coordination
- Information sharing

**Resource Usage** (typically 5-10%)
- Appropriate use of documents
- Research thoroughness

## Decision Analysis

### Decision-by-Decision Review

For each decision point:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Your Choice">
    **What You Selected**

    - Option chosen
    - Points awarded
    - When you decided
    - Time taken to decide
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Feedback">
    **Why That Score**

    - Explanation of consequences
    - What made it good/poor
    - What information supported it
    - What you might have missed
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Alternatives">
    **Other Options**

    - All available choices
    - Points for each
    - Why they scored differently
    - Best option highlighted
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Team Context">
    **Team Decision**

    - If it was team decision, how team voted
    - Discussion that occurred
    - Consensus or split
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Decision Patterns

Analysis of your decision-making style:

- **Risk Tolerance**: Conservative, Balanced, Aggressive
- **Speed vs Quality**: Fast decider or deliberate
- **Information Usage**: Data-driven or intuition
- **Collaboration**: Sought team input or independent

### Team Dynamics

Insights into how the team worked:

- **Leadership**: Did someone emerge as leader?
- **Role Clarity**: Did everyone understand their role?
- **Coordination**: How well did roles work together?
- **Conflict Resolution**: How disagreements were handled
- **Information Sharing**: Was information distributed effectively?

### Comparison

See how your team performed:

- **vs Other Teams**: If multiple teams ran the scenario
- **vs Benchmarks**: Average performance for this scenario
- **vs Expert Team**: Recommended approach

### Visual Playback

Watch the simulation unfold:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Timeline Replay">
    Click **Replay** in reports
  </Step>

  <Step title="Playback Controls">
    - Play/Pause
    - Speed (1x, 2x, 5x)
    - Jump to key moments
  </Step>

  <Step title="See Events">
    Events appear as they did during simulation
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Decisions">
    Your decisions highlighted with outcomes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Alternatives">
    Click to see what would have happened with different choices
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Key Moments

Reports highlight:

- **Turning Points**: When situation significantly changed
- **Critical Decisions**: Choices with biggest impact
- **Missed Opportunities**: Information or actions you overlooked
- **Strong Performances**: Decisions or actions that were exemplary

## Certificates

### Earning Certificates

Certificates awarded when:

- **Pass Threshold**: Minimum score achieved (e.g., 70%)
- **Completion**: Full simulation participated in
- **Requirements Met**: All mandatory decisions made

### Certificate Details

Includes:

- Participant name
- Simulation name and date
- Score achieved
- Skills practiced
- Issuing organization
- Unique verification code

### Downloading

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Report">
    Open simulation report
  </Step>

  <Step title="Certificate Section">
    Scroll to certificate area
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download">
    Click **Download Certificate** (PDF)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share">
    Add to LinkedIn, resume, training records
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Exporting Reports

### Individual Export

Download your performance data:

1. Open report
2. Click **Export**
3. Choose format: PDF, Excel, CSV
4. Select sections to include
5. Download

### Facilitator Bulk Export

Administrators can export all participant reports:

1. Open simulation
2. **Reports** > **Export All**
3. Select format
4. Generates zip file with all reports

## Sharing Reports

### Privacy Settings

Control who sees your performance:

- **Private**: Only you and facilitators
- **Team**: Your simulation team members
- **Organization**: All company members
- **Public**: Available to all (rare)

### Sharing Options

- **Direct Link**: Share report URL
- **PDF Export**: Email or print
- **Embed**: Add to learning management system
- **Leaderboard**: Opt-in to organization leaderboard

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Report not appearing">
    - Ensure simulation is completed (not just exited)
    - Check if scoring is still processing (can take a few minutes)
    - Refresh page
    - Contact facilitator if delayed beyond 1 hour
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Scores seem incorrect">
    - Review decision feedback to understand scoring
    - Check if there were deductions (e.g., time penalties)
    - Verify you're looking at individual vs team score
    - Contact facilitator if you believe there's an error
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't download certificate">
    - Verify you met passing threshold
    - Check browser pop-up blocker
    - Try different browser
    - Contact support for manual certificate generation
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Missing data in report">
    - Confirm you participated in entire simulation
    - Check if certain decisions were optional
    - Verify report has finished generating
    - Contact support if data truly missing
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
