# Simulation Execution

The execution phase is where participants actively engage with the crisis scenario, make decisions, and work together to manage the situation.

## What Happens During Execution

The active simulation includes:

- **Timeline Events**: News, messages, status updates appear over time
- **Decision Points**: Critical choices the team must make
- **Team Communication**: Real-time chat and coordination
- **Resource Access**: Documents, data, and information
- **AI Assistance**: Guidance from AI mentor (if enabled)
- **Progress Tracking**: Live dashboard showing status

## Responding to Events

### Types of Events

**News Updates**
- Breaking news reports
- Media coverage
- Public reaction
- External developments

**Incoming Messages**
- Emails from stakeholders
- Phone calls
- Text messages
- Social media mentions

**Status Changes**
- Crisis escalation/de-escalation
- Resource updates
- Casualties or damage reports
- Weather/environmental changes

### Event Actions

When events appear:

1. **Read Carefully**: Understand what's happening
2. **Extract Key Info**: Identify critical details
3. **Share with Team**: Communicate relevant information
4. **Consider Implications**: How does this affect decisions?
5. **Document**: Use notes feature to track important points

## Team Collaboration

### Effective Communication

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Coordinate Roles">
    Each team member monitors their area of responsibility and reports to the team.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Share Information">
    If you receive role-specific information, summarize key points for the team.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Discuss Decisions">
    Use chat to debate options before committing to team decisions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Assign Tasks">
    Delegate research or analysis to team members based on roles.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stay Organized">
    Use structured communication (e.g., "Update: [Topic]", "Decision needed: [Issue]")
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Chat Features

- **@Mentions**: Tag specific team members
- **Channels**: Separate conversations by topic
- **Reactions**: Quick acknowledgments
- **File Sharing**: Share screenshots or notes
- **Search**: Find past messages

## AI Mentor

### When to Use

The AI mentor can help with:

- **Understanding Events**: Clarify confusing information
- **Decision Guidance**: Suggest considerations (doesn't make decision for you)
- **Process Questions**: How features work
- **Best Practices**: Crisis management principles

### How to Interact

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Mentor Chat">
    Click mentor icon or access from chat panel
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Question">
    Type your question naturally
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Guidance">
    AI provides contextual advice
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply Insights">
    Use guidance to inform your decisions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The AI mentor provides guidance but won't tell you the "correct" answer. It's designed to help you think critically, not to do your work for you.
</Note>

## Time Management

### Pacing Strategies

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Early Phase">
    **First Third of Simulation**

    - Orient to situation
    - Establish team communication
    - Make initial assessments
    - Don't rush early decisions
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Middle Phase">
    **Second Third**

    - Crisis typically escalates
    - More frequent events
    - Time pressure increases
    - Balance speed with quality
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Late Phase">
    **Final Third**

    - Resolution opportunities
    - Reflect on earlier decisions
    - Final critical choices
    - Prepare for conclusion
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Managing Time Pressure

<Check>**Prioritize**: Focus on high-impact decisions</Check>
<Check>**Delegate**: Divide research tasks among team</Check>
<Check>**Set Internal Deadlines**: Decide before timer expires</Check>
<Check>**Avoid Paralysis**: Good decision now beats perfect decision too late</Check>
<Check>**Use Quick Votes**: Don't over-discuss obvious choices</Check>

## Facilitator Controls

### Monitoring the Simulation

Facilitators can see:

- **All Team Activity**: Decisions, chat, progress
- **Individual Contributions**: Who's active, who's quiet
- **Performance Metrics**: Real-time scoring
- **Technical Issues**: Connection problems, errors

## Participant Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stay Engaged">
    Actively participate throughout. Don't drift off even if things seem slow.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Think Critically">
    Analyze information, question assumptions, consider alternatives.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Communicate Clearly">
    Be concise and specific. In a crisis, clarity matters.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Respect Roles">
    Honor role assignments. Don't overstep or ignore your responsibilities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Learn from Mistakes">

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Support Your Team">

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Completion

### Automatic End

Simulation ends when:

- Scheduled duration reached
- Final event triggers
- All critical decisions made
- Facilitator manually ends it

### Transition to Debriefing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Execution Ends">
    Timer stops, no new events
  </Step>

  <Step title="Final Scoring">
    System calculates performance metrics
  </Step>

  <Step title="Message Appears">
    "Simulation Complete - Moving to Debriefing"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Auto-Redirect">
    Interface transitions to debriefing phase
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting During Execution

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Disconnected from simulation">
    - Check internet connection
    - Refresh browser
    - Rejoin simulation (progress should be saved)
    - Contact facilitator if unable to rejoin
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Events not appearing">
    - Verify simulation has started
    - Check timeline - events may not be scheduled yet
    - Refresh page
    - Alert facilitator if persistent
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't submit decision">
    - Check if time limit expired
    - Verify it's your role's decision
    - Ensure option is selected
    - Try refreshing and resubmitting
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Chat not working">
    - Check connection indicator
    - Refresh browser
    - Try logging out and back in
    - Use facilitator messaging if critical
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Team member not responding">
    - @Mention them directly
    - Contact via different channel if available
    - Alert facilitator
    - Team may need to proceed without them
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
