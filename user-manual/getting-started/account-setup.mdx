# Account Setup

Get started with crisisIQ by creating your account and completing the initial setup process.

## Creating Your Account

There are three ways to create a crisisIQ account:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Direct Registration" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up directly on the platform
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Invitation" icon="envelope">
    Accept an invitation from your organization
  </Card>
  <Card title="Join Code" icon="key">
    Use a code provided by your administrator
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Method 1: Direct Registration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Login Page">
    Navigate to the crisisIQ login page at your organization's URL or the public instance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Create Account'">
    Select the registration option to begin the signup process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Information">
    Provide the following details:
    - **Full Name**: Your first and last name
    - **Email Address**: A valid email you can access
    - **Password**: Must meet security requirements (see below)
    - **Company** (if applicable): Select or enter your organization
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Add additional information like profile picture, role, and preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

Your password must meet these criteria:

<Check>At least 8 characters long</Check>
<Check>Include at least one uppercase letter</Check>
<Check>Include at least one lowercase letter</Check>
<Check>Include at least one number</Check>
<Check>Include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)</Check>

<Warning>
Choose a strong, unique password. Avoid using personal information or common words.
</Warning>

## Method 2: Email Invitation

If your organization administrator has invited you:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Look for an invitation email from crisisIQ with the subject "You've been invited to join [Company Name] on crisisIQ"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the Invitation Link">
    The email contains a secure link to accept the invitation. Click it to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Password">
    Create a password for your account (must meet security requirements)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    If required, verify your email address by clicking the confirmation link sent to you
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Your Account">
    Once verified, you'll be logged in and can access your assigned role and simulations
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Invitation links expire after 7 days. If your link has expired, contact your administrator to resend the invitation.
</Info>

## Method 3: Join with Code

Some organizations use join codes for quick access:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Join Page">
    Go to the "Join with Code" option on the login screen
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the Join Code">
    Input the alphanumeric code provided by your organization administrator
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Your Details">
    Enter your name, email, and create a password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Verify your email and complete your profile setup
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First-Time Login

After creating your account, here's what to expect on your first login:

### 1. Dashboard Tour (Optional)

New users may see a quick tour highlighting key features:
- Navigation menu
- Active simulations
- Quick actions
- Profile settings

### 2. Profile Completion

Complete your user profile with:
- **Profile Photo** (optional but recommended)
- **Job Title/Role**
- **Department** (if applicable)
- **Preferred Language** (German or English)
- **Notification Preferences**

### 3. Company Selection

If you belong to multiple organizations:
1. Use the **Company Switcher** in the top navigation
2. Select the organization you want to work with
3. Your dashboard will update to show that company's content

<Note>
Your role and permissions may differ between companies. The available features depend on your role in the selected company.
</Note>

## Initial Configuration

### Language Preference

Set your preferred language:

1. Click your **profile icon** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Language** from the dropdown
3. Choose between **German (DE)** or **English (EN)**
4. The interface will update immediately

### Notification Settings

Configure how you receive updates:

- **Email Notifications**: Simulation invites, deadlines, results
- **In-App Notifications**: Real-time updates while logged in
- **Push Notifications**: Available when installed as PWA

## Account Security

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For enhanced security, enable 2FA in your account settings:

1. Go to **Profile** > **Security Settings**
2. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code to confirm

<Tip>
We recommend using apps like Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password for 2FA.
</Tip>

### Session Management

- **Auto-logout**: Sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity
- **Multiple devices**: You can be logged in on multiple devices
- **Secure connections**: All data is encrypted using HTTPS/WSS

## Troubleshooting Account Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive the verification email">
    1. Check your spam/junk folder
    2. Verify you entered the correct email address
    3. Request a new verification email from the login page
    4. Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My invitation link has expired">
    Contact your organization administrator to resend the invitation. Invitation links are valid for 7 days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't set my password">
    Ensure your password meets all requirements:
    - At least 8 characters
    - Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
    - Not a commonly used password
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I belong to multiple companies">
    Use the Company Switcher in the top navigation to switch between organizations. Your role may differ in each company.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email verification is not working">
    1. Ensure the link hasn't expired (valid for 24 hours)
    2. Try copying and pasting the URL directly into your browser
    3. Request a new verification email
    4. Contact support@crisisiq.com for assistance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
