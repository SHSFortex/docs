# Welcome to crisisIQ

crisisIQ is a cutting-edge crisis simulation training platform that uses artificial intelligence to help organizations and individuals prepare for real-world crisis scenarios.

## What is crisisIQ?

crisisIQ provides interactive, scenario-based training that simulates crisis situations in a safe, controlled environment. Whether you're training a team or developing individual skills, crisisIQ offers:

- **Realistic Scenarios**: Experience authentic crisis situations with dynamic events and decision points
- **AI-Powered Mentoring**: Get real-time guidance from our AI mentor during simulations
- **Team Collaboration**: Work together with your team in group simulations
- **Solo Training**: Practice individually with personalized solo training scenarios
- **Performance Analytics**: Receive detailed reports and insights on your decision-making

## Who is crisisIQ For?

### Organizations
- **Company Administrators**: Manage users, create teams, and oversee training programs
- **Content Creators**: Design custom scenarios and training materials
- **Team Leaders**: Monitor participant progress and analyze team performance

### Individuals
- **Participants**: Engage in group simulations with your team
- **Solo Learners**: Practice crisis management skills independently
