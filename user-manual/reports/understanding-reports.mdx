## Report Sections

### Performance Summary

Top-level overview:

- **Overall Score**: Total points earned
- **Grade**: Letter grade or Pass/Fail
- **Percentile**: Ranking vs others
- **Completion Time**: Duration
- **Certification Status**: Earned or not

### Decision Analysis

Breakdown of each decision:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Your Choices">
    - Options selected
    - Points earned
    - Response time
    - Rationale (if provided)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Feedback">
    - Why scored as it did
    - Consequences
    - Optimal choice
    - Learning points
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Alternatives">
    - Other options available
    - Points for each
    - Outcomes explained
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Skill Assessment

Performance across competencies:

- Decision-Making Quality
- Risk Assessment
- Stakeholder Management
- Communication
- Resource Management
- Compliance & Procedures

Each rated: Novice → Competent → Proficient → Expert

### Timeline Replay

Visual playback showing:

- Events as they occurred
- Your decisions highlighted
- Consequences shown
- Alternative paths revealed

## Interpreting Scores

### Score Components

Typical weighting:

- **Decision Quality** (60-70%): Optimal choices
- **Response Time** (10-20%): Speed balanced with accuracy
- **Resource Usage** (10-20%): Accessed helpful documents
- **Completion** (5-10%): Finished all decisions

### What Scores Mean

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="< 50%">
    **Developing**

    - Still learning concepts
    - More practice needed
    - Review feedback carefully
    - Consider foundational training
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="50-70%">
    **Passing**

    - Basic understanding demonstrated
    - Room for improvement
    - Focus on weak areas
    - Good foundation to build on
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="71-85%">
    **Competent**

    - Solid performance
    - Good grasp of principles
    - Minor improvements possible
    - Ready for more challenge
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="86-95%">
    **Proficient**

    - Strong performance
    - Excellent decision-making
    - Deep understanding
    - Consider mentoring others
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="96-100%">
    **Expert**

    - Exceptional performance
    - Mastery demonstrated
    - Role model for others
    - Ready for advanced scenarios
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Feedback Types

### Automated Feedback

AI-generated insights:

**Strengths:**
- Decisions you excelled at
- Patterns of good performance
- Skills demonstrated well

**Opportunities:**
- Common mistakes
- Skills needing development
- Areas for focus

**Recommendations:**
- Next scenarios to try
- Training resources
- Skill-building activities

### Facilitator Feedback

Human observations (when available):

- Specific commendations
- Constructive suggestions
- Context for decisions
- Team dynamics notes

## Using Reports for Growth

### Reflection Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Overall">
    Look at summary score and grade
  </Step>

  <Step title="Identify Patterns">
    What types of decisions went well/poorly?
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read Feedback">
    Understand the "why" behind scores
  </Step>

  <Step title="Note Surprises">
    What unexpected or contradicted assumptions?
  </Step>

  <Step title="Extract Lessons">
    Key takeaways for future situations
  </Step>

  <Step title="Plan Action">
    Specific steps to improve
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Creating Action Plans

Based on reports:

1. **Identify Top 2-3 Gaps**: Focus areas
2. **Set Measurable Goals**: "Improve X from 60% to 80%"
3. **Choose Practice**: Scenarios targeting those skills
4. **Study Resources**: Read relevant materials
5. **Track Progress**: Compare future reports

## Comparing Performance

### Personal Benchmarks

Track improvement:

- **First vs Retry**: Measure learning
- **Over Time**: Long-term growth
- **By Category**: Where you excel/struggle
- **Against Goals**: Meeting targets?

### Anonymous Comparisons

(If enabled):

- Organization average
- Peer group (similar role/experience)
- Top performers (90th percentile)
- Expert benchmarks

<Note>
Comparisons are anonymous. Focus on personal growth, not just ranking.
</Note>

## Certificates

### Earning Criteria

Typically requires:

- Minimum score (70-80%+)
- Full completion
- All required decisions made
- Scenario configured for certificates

### Certificate Contents

- Participant name
- Scenario details
- Score achieved
- Date completed
- Skills practiced
- Verification code

## Exporting & Sharing

### Export Options

- **PDF**: Full formatted report
- **CSV**: Data for analysis
- **Print**: Hard copy

### Sharing

Control visibility:

- **Private**: Only you and facilitators
- **Team**: Simulation participants
- **Organization**: Company members
- **Public**: Rarely used
