# Invitations & Join Codes

Learn how to join crisisIQ through email invitations or organization join codes.

## Types of Invitations

There are three ways to join a crisisIQ organization:

  </Card>
  <Card title="Join Code" icon="key">

## Email Invitations

Organization administrators can invite you via email to join their crisisIQ organization.

### Receiving an Invitation

When invited, you'll receive an email with:

- **Subject**: "You've been invited to join [Company Name] on crisisIQ"
- **From**: noreply@crisisiq.com
- **Invitation Details**: Company name, your assigned role, inviter's name
- **Accept Link**: Unique URL to accept the invitation
- **Expiration**: Invitation valid for **7 days**

### Accepting an Email Invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Look for the invitation email (check spam/junk folders too)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Details">
    Read the invitation to understand:
    - Which organization is inviting you
    - Your assigned role (Participant, Content Creator, Admin)
    - Who sent the invitation
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Accept Invitation'">
    Click the acceptance link in the email
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create/Login Account">
    **If you don't have an account:**
    - Enter your name
    - Create a password
    - Accept terms of service

    **If you already have an account:**
    - Log in with your existing credentials
    - The organization will be added to your account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email (if new account)">
    Complete email verification if required
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Organization">
    You'll be directed to your dashboard with access to the new organization
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
If you already have a crisisIQ account, accepting an invitation adds the new organization to your existing account. You can switch between organizations using the Company Switcher.
</Info>

## Join Codes

Join codes provide a quick way to join an organization without requiring a direct email invitation.

### What is a Join Code?

- **Format**: 8-12 character alphanumeric code (e.g., `ABC123XY`)
- **Shared By**: Organization administrators
- **Purpose**: Bulk onboarding, workshop participants, temporary access
- **Expiration**: Can be set by administrator (default: no expiration)

### Using a Join Code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Join Page">
    On the crisisIQ login screen, click **"Join with Code"**

    Or visit: `https://crisisiq.com/join-with-code`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the Code">
    Type or paste the join code provided by your organization
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Verify Code'">
    The system will validate the code and display the organization name
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Your Details">
    **If you don't have an account:**
    - Enter your full name
    - Provide your email address
    - Create a password
    - Accept terms of service

    **If you have an account:**
    - Log in with your existing credentials
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Joining">
    Review the organization details and click **"Join Organization"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email (if new account)">
    Check your email for a verification link
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Using crisisIQ">
    Access your new organization's dashboard and features
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Join Code Advantages

<Check>**Fast onboarding** - No waiting for email</Check>
<Check>**Bulk enrollment** - Share one code with many users</Check>
<Check>**Flexible sharing** - Via email, chat, presentation, etc.</Check>
<Check>**No email required** - Users can discover and join</Check>

## Simulation-Specific Invitations

Participants may receive direct links to join a specific simulation.

### Simulation Invitation Email

When invited to a simulation, you'll receive:

- **Subject**: "You're invited to participate in [Simulation Name]"
- **From**: noreply@crisisiq.com
- **Simulation Details**: Title, description, start time
- **Join Link**: Direct link to the simulation
- **Requirements**: May require email verification to join

### Joining a Simulation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Simulation Link">
    Open the invitation email and click the **"Join Simulation"** link
  </Step>

  <Step title="Login or Register">
    - **Existing users**: Log in
    - **New users**: Create an account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email (if required)">
    Some simulations require verified email addresses
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Simulation">
    You'll be directed to the simulation preparation or briefing page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Start">
    Follow the simulation timeline (preparation → briefing → execution → debriefing)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Simulation invitations may have specific start times. Join early to complete the preparation phase.
</Note>

## Invitation Status

### Checking Pending Invitations

To see invitations you haven't accepted yet:

1. Log in to your crisisIQ account
2. Look for a notification badge or banner
3. Or go to **Profile** > **Invitations**
4. Review pending invitations and accept/decline

### Managing Multiple Organizations

If you belong to multiple organizations:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Company Switcher">
    **Switch Between Organizations**

    1. Click the **Company Switcher** in the top navigation
    2. View all organizations you belong to
    3. Select the one you want to work with
    4. Dashboard updates to show that company's content
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Different Roles">
    **Role-Based Access**

    - You may have different roles in different organizations
    - Permissions and features vary by role
    - Company Switcher shows your role in each organization
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Invitation Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invitation email not received">
    1. Check spam/junk folders
    2. Verify the administrator sent it to the correct email
    3. Whitelist noreply@crisisiq.com
    4. Ask the administrator to resend the invitation
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invitation link says 'expired'">
    Email invitations expire after 7 days. Contact the administrator to send a new invitation or request a join code instead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invitation link says 'invalid'">
    - The invitation may have been revoked
    - It might have already been used
    - The link could be malformed (ensure you copied the entire URL)
    - Request a new invitation from your administrator
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Join code not working">
    - Verify you entered the code correctly (case-sensitive)
    - Check if the code has expired
    - Ask the administrator if the code is still active
    - Ensure you're on the correct crisisIQ instance
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Already have an account with different email">
    If you want to accept an invitation with a different email:
    - Create a new account with the invited email, OR
    - Contact the administrator to resend the invitation to your existing email
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invitation accepted but can't see organization">
    - Log out and log back in
    - Check the Company Switcher dropdown
    - Contact your administrator to verify your access
    - Clear browser cache and try again
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Declining Invitations

If you receive an invitation you don't want to accept:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Invitation Link">
    Open the email and click the link
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose 'Decline'">
    Instead of accepting, click **"Decline Invitation"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Decline">
    Confirm that you don't want to join this organization
  </Step>

  <Step title="Notification Sent">
    The administrator may be notified of your decline (depending on settings)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Declining an invitation doesn't prevent you from joining later via a join code or new invitation.
</Note>
