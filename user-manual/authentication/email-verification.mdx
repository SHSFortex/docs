# Email Verification

Email verification ensures the security of your account and enables full access to crisisIQ features.

## Why Verify Your Email?

Email verification is required to:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Security" icon="shield-check">
    Confirms you own the email address
  </Card>
  <Card title="Communication" icon="envelope">
    Enables important notifications and updates
  </Card>
  <Card title="Password Recovery" icon="key">
    Allows password reset if you forget it
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full Access" icon="unlock">
    Required for joining simulations
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
Some features, especially **joining participant simulations**, require a verified email address. You may see limited functionality until verification is complete.
</Warning>

## Verification Process

### For New Accounts

When you create a new account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register Your Account">
    Complete the registration form with your email and other details
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Look for a verification email from crisisIQ in your inbox
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Verification Link">
    Open the email and click the **"Verify Email Address"** button or link
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirmation">
    You'll be redirected to crisisIQ with a success message
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Granted">
    Your account is now fully activated and you can access all features
  </Step>
</Steps>

### For Invited Users

If you were invited by an organization:

1. Accept the invitation via the email link
2. Set your password
3. A verification email will be sent automatically
4. Click the verification link to complete setup

## Verification Email

### What to Expect

The verification email contains:

- **Subject**: "Verify your crisisIQ email address"
- **From**: noreply@crisisiq.com
- **Verification Link**: Unique, time-limited URL
- **Validity**: Link expires after **24 hours**

### Email Sample

```
Subject: Verify your crisisIQ email address

Hello [Your Name],

Welcome to crisisIQ! Please verify your email address to activate your account.

[Verify Email Address Button]

Or copy and paste this link:
https://crisisiq.com/verify-email?token=abc123...

This link expires in 24 hours.

If you didn't create this account, please ignore this email.

- The crisisIQ Team
```

## Resending Verification Email

If you didn't receive the verification email or it expired:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login Attempt">
    Try to log in to your crisisIQ account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verification Notice">
    You'll see a message: **"Email verification required"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Resend Request">
    Click the **"Resend Verification Email"** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Inbox">
    A new verification email will be sent within minutes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify">
    Click the link in the new email to complete verification
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can request a new verification email up to **5 times per hour** to prevent abuse.
</Note>

## Email Not Arriving?

If the verification email doesn't appear in your inbox:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check spam/junk folder">
    Email filters may have redirected the message. Check:
    - Spam folder
    - Junk folder
    - Promotions tab (Gmail)
    - Updates tab (Gmail)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wait a few minutes">
    Email delivery can take 2-5 minutes. Be patient before requesting a resend.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Verify email address">
    Log out and try to log back in. The system will show you which email address needs verification. Ensure it's correct.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Whitelist the sender">
    Add these addresses to your email whitelist/safe senders:
    - noreply@crisisiq.com
    - support@crisisiq.com
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Check email filters">
    Some corporate email systems have aggressive filters. Contact your IT department to whitelist crisisIQ emails.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Try different email">
    If you used a disposable or temporary email service, it may not be receiving messages. Contact support to update your account email.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Verification Status

### Check Your Status

To see if your email is verified:

1. Log in to crisisIQ (if possible)
2. Go to **Profile Settings**
3. Look for **Email Status** indicator

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Verified ✓">
    **Status**: Email Verified

    - Green checkmark next to email
    - Full access to all features
    - Can join simulations
    - Receives all notifications
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Pending ✗">
    **Status**: Verification Pending

    - Orange warning icon
    - Limited feature access
    - Cannot join participant simulations
    - Resend verification option available
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Unverified Account Restrictions

Until you verify your email, you may experience:

<Warning>
- **Cannot join simulations** as a participant
- **Limited notifications** (in-app only)
- **No password reset** capability
- **Restricted invitations** to other users
</Warning>

Administrative and content creation features may still be accessible depending on your organization's settings.

## Changing Your Email

If you need to change your email address:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Update in Profile">
    Go to Profile Settings and update your email address
  </Step>

  <Step title="New Verification Required">
    A verification email will be sent to the new address
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify New Email">
    Click the link in the email sent to your new address
  </Step>

  <Step title="Old Email Deactivated">
    Your old email will be replaced once the new one is verified
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your old email remains active until you verify the new one. This prevents accidental lockout.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Verification link says 'expired'">
    Links expire after 24 hours. Log in and request a new verification email from the verification prompt page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Verification link says 'invalid'">
    The link may have been already used or malformed. Request a new verification email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Clicking link does nothing">
    - Try copying and pasting the full URL into your browser
    - Ensure you're using an up-to-date browser
    - Check if browser extensions are interfering
    - Try in a different browser or incognito mode
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Still showing unverified after clicking">
    - Clear your browser cache and cookies
    - Log out and log back in
    - Wait a few minutes for the system to update
    - Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't access email account">
    If you no longer have access to your registered email:
    - Contact your organization administrator
    - They can update your email address
    - You'll receive a verification email at the new address
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Company email blocking messages">
    Some corporate email systems block automated emails:
    - Ask your IT department to whitelist crisisIQ
    - Provide them with: noreply@crisisiq.com and support@crisisiq.com
    - Alternatively, use a personal email if allowed by your organization
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
