# Logging In to crisis IQ

Learn how to access your crisisIQ account and manage your login sessions.

## Standard Login

Access crisisIQ using your email and password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Login Page">
    Visit your organization's crisisIQ URL or the public instance login page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    - **Email**: The email address associated with your account
    - **Password**: Your account password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Login'">
    Submit your credentials to access the platform
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete 2FA (if enabled)">
    If two-factor authentication is enabled, enter the verification code from your authenticator app
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your session will remain active for 24 hours of inactivity. After that, you'll need to log in again.
</Info>

## Remember Me

The "Remember Me" option keeps you logged in across browser sessions:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Enabled">
    **Remember Me Checked**

    - Stays logged in even after closing the browser
    - Session persists for up to 30 days
    - Recommended for personal devices only
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Disabled">
    **Remember Me Unchecked**

    - Session ends when browser is closed
    - More secure for shared/public computers
    - Recommended for work or public devices
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
Never use "Remember Me" on shared or public computers for security reasons.
</Warning>

## Login Security

### Secure Connection

crisisIQ uses industry-standard security measures:

<Check>**HTTPS Encryption** - All data encrypted in transit</Check>
<Check>**Secure WebSockets (WSS)** - Real-time features protected</Check>
<Check>**JWT Tokens** - Secure session management</Check>
<Check>**Token Expiration** - Automatic logout after 24 hours</Check>

### Failed Login Attempts

Security measures for failed login attempts:

- **3 failed attempts**: Warning message displayed
- **5 failed attempts**: 5-minute lockout
- **10 failed attempts**: 30-minute lockout
- **Continued failures**: Account temporarily suspended

<Info>
If your account is locked due to multiple failed attempts, wait for the lockout period to expire or use the "Forgot Password" option to reset your password.
</Info>

## Session Management

### Active Sessions

Your account can have multiple active sessions:

- Desktop browser
- Mobile browser
- PWA installation
- Tablet device

All sessions share the same authentication token and will be logged out simultaneously when you explicitly log out.

### Auto-Logout

You'll be automatically logged out if:

- You explicitly click "Logout"
- Your session expires (24 hours of inactivity)
- Your authentication token is revoked
- You change your password
- An administrator deactivates your account

### Staying Logged In

To prevent auto-logout:

- Interact with the platform regularly
- Keep the browser tab/window open
- Install as PWA for persistent sessions

## Company Context

If you belong to multiple organizations:

### Company Switcher

1. Look for the **Company Switcher** dropdown in the top navigation
2. Click to see all companies you belong to
3. Select the company you want to work with
4. Your dashboard and permissions update automatically

<Note>
Your role and available features may differ between companies. Switching companies updates your context without requiring re-login.
</Note>

### Last Selected Company

The platform remembers your last selected company:

- Automatically loads that company on next login
- Persists across sessions
- Can be changed anytime via the Company Switcher

## Language Selection

Change the interface language at login or anytime after:

1. Click the **Language Selector** (flag icon)
2. Choose **Deutsch (DE)** or **English (EN)**
3. Interface updates immediately

Your language preference is saved to your account.

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password">
    Use the [Forgot Password](/user-manual/authentication/password-reset) feature to reset it. See the Password Reset guide for detailed instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My email and password don't work">
    Verify that:
    - You're using the correct email address
    - Caps Lock is not enabled
    - You're on the correct crisisIQ instance URL
    - Your account hasn't been deactivated

    If issues persist, contact your administrator or support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm getting 'Account locked' message">
    This occurs after multiple failed login attempts. Wait for the lockout period (5-30 minutes) or reset your password.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email verification required">
    If you see a message about email verification, check your inbox for the verification email. See [Email Verification](/user-manual/authentication/email-verification) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Two-factor code not working">
    Ensure:
    - Your device clock is synchronized
    - You're using the latest code from your authenticator app
    - The code hasn't expired (codes refresh every 30 seconds)

    If 2FA continues to fail, contact your administrator to reset it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Session expires too quickly">
    Standard sessions last 24 hours with activity. If you're being logged out prematurely:
    - Check if "Remember Me" is enabled
    - Verify your browser allows cookies and local storage
    - Check for browser extensions that clear cookies
    - Ensure you're not in private/incognito mode
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Security Recommendations

<Check>Use a strong, unique password</Check>
<Check>Enable two-factor authentication</Check>
<Check>Never share your credentials</Check>
<Check>Log out from shared/public computers</Check>
<Check>Keep your browser updated</Check>
<Check>Use "Remember Me" only on personal devices</Check>

### Password Management

- Use a password manager (e.g., 1Password, LastPass, Bitwarden)
- Change your password if you suspect it's been compromised
- Don't reuse passwords from other services
- Update your password periodically

### When to Contact Support

Reach out to support@crisisiq.com if:

- You can't access your account after password reset
- Your account appears compromised
- You're unable to receive verification emails
- You experience repeated technical login issues
