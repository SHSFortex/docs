# Android Installation

Install crisisIQ on your Android phone or tablet for an app-like experience with offline capabilities and home screen access.

## What is PWA Installation?

Progressive Web App installation provides:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Native App Feel" icon="mobile">
    Runs like installed app
  </Card>
  <Card title="Home Screen Access" icon="grid">
    Launch from app drawer or home screen
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full Screen" icon="expand">
    No browser address bar
  </Card>
  <Card title="Offline Mode" icon="wifi-slash">
    Access content without internet
  </Card>
  <Card title="Push Notifications" icon="bell-on">
    Get alerts when app is closed
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fast Performance" icon="gauge-high">
    Cached for quick loading
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Requirements

<Check>**Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later** recommended</Check>
<Check>**Chrome, Edge, or Samsung Internet browser**</Check>
<Check>**Internet connection** (for installation)</Check>
<Check>**Storage**: ~50-100MB available</Check>

## Installation Steps

### Method 1: Chrome (Most Common)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chrome Browser">
    Launch Google Chrome on your Android device
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to crisisIQ">
    Go to your organization's crisisIQ URL
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In">
    Sign in to your account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Look for Install Prompt">
    A banner may appear at bottom: **"Add crisisIQ to Home screen"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap 'Install' or 'Add'">
    Follow the prompt to install
  </Step>

  <Step title="Or Use Menu Method">
    If no prompt: Tap **⋮** (three dots) > **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Installation">
    Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"** in the dialog
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find App">
    Look for crisisIQ icon in app drawer or home screen
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch">
    Tap icon to open in full-screen app mode
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: Samsung Internet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Samsung Internet">
    Launch Samsung Internet browser
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate and Log In">
    Go to crisisIQ URL and sign in
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Menu">
    Tap **☰** (three lines) in bottom-right
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select 'Add Page To'">
    Choose **"Add page to"** option
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose 'Home Screen'">
    Select **"Home screen"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize (Optional)">
    Edit app name if desired
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add">
    Tap **"Add"** to complete
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 3: Edge Browser

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Microsoft Edge">
    Launch Edge browser
  </Step>

  <Step title="Visit crisisIQ">
    Navigate to URL and log in
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Menu">
    Tap **⋯** (three dots) at bottom
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select 'Add to Phone'">
    Choose **"Add to phone"** or **"Install"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install">
    Tap **"Install"** to confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Detailed Installation Guide

### Finding the Install Prompt

**Chrome Install Banner:**

- Appears at bottom of screen
- Shows crisisIQ logo
- Text: "Add crisisIQ to Home screen"
- Buttons: "Cancel" and "Install"

**If Banner Doesn't Appear:**

1. Wait a few seconds (banner may be delayed)
2. Use manual menu method (see above)
3. Ensure you're logged in
4. Try refreshing the page

### Manual Installation via Menu

If automatic prompt doesn't appear:

**Chrome:**
1. Tap **⋮** (top-right)
2. Scroll to find **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
3. Tap it
4. Confirm

**Samsung Internet:**
1. Tap **☰** (bottom-right)
2. Tap **"Add page to"**
3. Select **"Home screen"**
4. Confirm

## After Installation

### Finding the App

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="App Drawer">
    **All Apps List**

    - Swipe up from home screen (most launchers)
    - Find "crisisIQ" alphabetically
    - Tap to launch
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Home Screen">
    **Quick Access**

    - Icon added to home screen automatically (usually)
    - If not, drag from app drawer to home screen
    - Place in folder if desired
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Recent Apps">
    **Multitasking**

    - Appears in recent apps like native apps
    - Swipe through to find it
    - Switch between apps normally
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### First Launch

When you first open the installed app:

1. **Splash Screen**: crisisIQ logo (brief)
2. **Login Screen**: May need to log in again
3. **Permissions**: Allow notifications if prompted
4. **Offline Data**: May ask to cache data
5. **Dashboard**: Loads your personal dashboard

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Works Offline">

    - View cached dashboard
    - Access training history
    - Read downloaded certificates
    - Continue saved scenarios (limited)
    - Browse cached resources

### Notifications

Enable push notifications:

1. Allow when prompted on first launch
2. Or: Android Settings > Apps > crisisIQ > Notifications
3. Toggle on desired notification types:
   - Simulation reminders
   - Results available
   - Team messages
   - Achievements

## Managing the App

### App Settings

Access via:

- **In-App**: Profile > Settings
- **Android Settings**: Settings > Apps > crisisIQ

**Available Options:**
- Notifications
- Storage usage
- Permissions
- Mobile data usage
- Battery optimization
- Default app settings

### Updating the App

PWA apps update automatically:

- No Google Play Store updates
- Updates download when online
- Latest version always available
- May prompt to reload if major update

### Moving Icon

- Long-press icon
- Drag to new location on home screen
- Or drag to app drawer
- Create folders by dragging onto other apps

### Uninstalling

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-Press Icon">
    Hold crisisIQ icon
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Uninstall">
    Tap **"Uninstall"** or drag to **"Remove"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm removal
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Uninstalling the PWA doesn't delete your account. You can still access via browser or reinstall.
</Note>

## Optimization Tips

### For Best Performance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Allow Background Data">
    Settings > Apps > crisisIQ > Mobile data & WiFi > Allow background data

    Enables notifications and content syncing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Disable Battery Optimization">
    Settings > Apps > crisisIQ > Battery > Unrestricted

    Prevents Android from killing the app
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Grant Permissions">
    Allow notifications, storage as prompted

    Full feature access
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep Storage Free">
    Maintain at least 500MB free space

    Smooth operation and caching
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Data Usage

Control mobile data:

- **WiFi Only**: Settings > crisisIQ > Mobile data (toggle off)
- **Limit Background**: Restrict background data usage
- **Monitor**: Check data usage in Android settings

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't find install option">
    **Causes:**
    - Already installed (check app drawer)
    - Browser doesn't support PWA (use Chrome)
    - Not logged in

    **Solutions:**
    - Check all home screens and app drawer
    - Switch to Chrome browser
    - Log in before attempting install
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="App opens in browser instead of full-screen">
    **Cause:** Clicking links from external sources

    **Solution:**
    - Launch from home screen icon, not links
    - External links open in browser
    - Copy URL and open in installed app
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notifications not working">
    **Solutions:**
    - Settings > Apps > crisisIQ > Notifications (enable)
    - Check Do Not Disturb isn't active
    - Ensure battery optimization not blocking
    - Re-launch app and allow when prompted
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="App is slow">
    **Solutions:**
    - Close and reopen app
    - Clear browser cache (Chrome > Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data)
    - Restart device
    - Ensure sufficient storage
    - Uninstall and reinstall
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Offline mode not syncing">
    **Solutions:**
    - Connect to WiFi
    - Open app to trigger sync
    - Check background data is enabled
    - Force close and reopen app
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
